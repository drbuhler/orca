A House With a Story

498 Orcas Street is a one-of-a-kind coastal property in Morro Bay — panoramic ocean views, a half-mile walk to the beach, and a view of Morro Rock from the kitchen sink. It's the kind of home you work your whole life to call your own.

But as of late 2025, it needed work. Abandoned mid-project, failing wrap, a challenge no ordinary homebuyer would touch. We saw something different: the legacy of a builder with a unique vision, and a home that deserves respect, restoration, and a new owner who will love it.

Our team has 20 deals under our belt — a 26-unit apartment, a CA forest house, almost a dozen flips. With the right partners, mindset, and contractors, we're restoring this home and sharing the whole journey right here on this site.
