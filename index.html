A House With a Story

498 Orcas Street is a one-of-a-kind coastal property in Morro Bay — panoramic ocean views, a half-mile walk to the beach, and a view of Morro Rock while cooking dinner. It's the kind of home you work your whole life to call your own!

As of late 2025, it needed work. It was abandoned, demolished, and wasting away. We saw the legacy that could be lovingly restored with enough vision and hard work. This blog follows our journey. Join us!
